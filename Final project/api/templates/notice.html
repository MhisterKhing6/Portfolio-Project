INCOME GENERATION

platform gives each user 150 cedis as a welcome bonus apart from that there are two main ways to get income.

Through Income from shops

Once and Investor purchase a shop there is and income that will be generated daily by trading.
A Shop owner can trade once a day and the owner must trade every day to get income. Not shop Income is not automatically generated the user must log into the platform to trade before income will be generated

Through Underlines purchasing higher shops

There is no registration bonus for inviting investors who trade at shop level zero, but there is a bonus that is given to the superior incase and underline upgrades to higher shop. The bonus is at level one. That means if A invites B and B invites C in the case C upgrade to a higher shop B will receive the bonus and it ends there. There is no Limit to the Number of Users you can Invite.

Withdrawal Information

Withdrawal from the Platform is through mobile money and users at shop level zero cant withdraw. There is no limit to the amount you can withdraw, however withdrawal is once every 15 days .
  • STEPS
  • Bind Withdrawal Information in the Profile Page
  • Click on The withdrawal Link
  • Enter The Amount you want to withdraw and submit.
    Withdrawal will reflect within 24 hours

Deposit Information

Funding your account is through MTN MOMO now. There is no limit to the amount you can deposit, deposited amount will reflect on the user platform with 10mins .
  • STEPS
  • Click on the Deposit Link
  • Enter the amount you want to deposit and choose vendor (ONLY MTN AVAILABLE NOW)
  • Submit the request
  • A page will be generated with the number and reference to send the number to base on the vendor.
  • Send the money with the exact amount to that number
  • Copy the transaction Id to the transaction field on the page and click done paying
    • Deposit will reflect within 10 minutes
